Who We Are

About KRIoTIC

The KUET Robotics and IoT Innovation Club — where engineers, makers, and dreamers unite.

Our Story

KRIoTIC was founded by a passionate group of KUET students who believed that hands-on innovation was the best way to learn. Starting with a handful of members and a shared workspace, the club quickly grew into one of the most vibrant technical communities at KUET.

Today, we are home to hundreds of members working across Robotics, IoT, AI, Machine Learning, Computer Vision, Embedded Systems, and beyond. We host workshops, hackathons, competitions, and research sessions year-round.

Our Mission

To empower KUET students with practical knowledge and experience in robotics, IoT, and emerging technologies — enabling them to build real-world solutions and compete at the national and international level.

Our Vision

To become the leading student-driven technology innovation hub in Bangladesh, recognized for producing skilled engineers, groundbreaking projects, and a culture of continuous learning.

Our Values

Collaboration, creativity, inclusivity, and a relentless drive to push the boundaries of what's possible — these are the values that define our community.

Core Areas

Technologies and domains we specialize in

Robotics

Autonomous systems, line followers, drone development, and robotic arms.

Internet of Things

Smart home systems, environmental monitoring, and industrial IoT.

Artificial Intelligence

Deep learning, computer vision, natural language processing, and reinforcement learning.

Embedded Systems

Arduino, STM32, Raspberry Pi, FPGA, and low-level firmware development.

Mechatronics

Servo-motor control, pneumatic systems, and electromechanical integration.

R&D

Research publications, prototype development, and industry collaboration.
